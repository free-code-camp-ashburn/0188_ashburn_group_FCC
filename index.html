Get Started

The single fastest way to connect with us on is Flock!

Step 1: Join Flock

Flock is a slack-alternative and the premier medium where FCCA Members keep in touch!

Channels:

  • Announcements - Important channel-wide announcements from the admins!
  • General - Make new friends by chatting about coding, events, and related topics!
  • Resources & Local Events - Share your best finds for great resources and cool events!
  • Daily-Log - Daily coding is a powerful habit. Strengthen this habit by typing 1 sentence about what you coded today!

    About

    We are a local, organized group of aspiring developers who strive to learn by working together.

    We code together at Starbucks on the weekend and go to web development events. We communicate through an app called Flock.
